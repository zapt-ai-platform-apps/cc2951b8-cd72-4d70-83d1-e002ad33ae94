# Help and Support

This journey provides guidance on how to access help and support within MotoConnect.

1. **Accessing Support**
   - Log in to your dashboard.
   - A Customer Support Chat widget is available if you are logged in.

2. **Using the Chat Widget**
   - Click on the chat icon to open the chat interface.
   - Start a conversation with our support team.
   - The chat widget is only available when you are logged in to ensure personalized assistance.

3. **Alternative Support Channels**
   - Alternatively, you can contact support via email or phone, as listed in the dashboard.
   - Detailed error messages guide you through troubleshooting common issues.

4. **Feedback**
   - Provide feedback directly through the chat or support portal to help us improve the service.
